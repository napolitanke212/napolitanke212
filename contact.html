CONTACT

Find out how to get in touch with us below. If you have questions please refer to us, please don’t hesitate to get in contact with us.

  • WHEN WILL MY ORDER BE SHIPPED?
    • All orders will be processed as soon as possible within standard business hours,
      if an order is placed over a weekend or public holiday process will begin on the next working day,
      in some cases this could take up to 36 hours.
      Standard delivery times do not include any time for processing your order.
      Once your order has been shipped you will receive a notification of dispatch including an online tracking number.
  • HOW CAN I RETURN MY ORDER FOR AN EXCHANGE?
    • Size exchanges can be made in any of our stores, provided we have the stock to accommodate; alternatively, you can ship the item back to us.
    • Please ensure you return the item within 14 days of receipt for any change of mind exchange/return. Please ensure the goods are unused in original condition/packaging, tags attached and accompanied with a receipt for any change of mind/return. Area 51 will not be held accountable for any packages we do not ship ourselves; the customer must pay for shipping costs.
    • Any exchange/return for change of mind will be processed within 5 working days and we will inform you once your return has been processed.
    • All international exchanges will incur an additional shipping charge which you will need to pay before we can process an exchange. For Australia this is $25 and international orders are $65. For all New Zealand orders, the additional shipping fee is waived.
  • HOW CAN I RETURN MY ORDER FOR A REFUND?
    • Limited release products are not returnable for refund Sale items are not returnable for refund Item(s) must be accompanied by a relevant sales invoice and filled out returns form Item(s) must be unworn and in as new condition with tags attached/footwear must be returned in its original box with protective packaging as the box is considered part of the product Item(s) are returned within 14 days of purchase.
    • Any return delivery fees are at the customers expense.
    • A shipping and handling fee of $5.00 will be deducted for any change of mind refunds, alternatively we are happy to provide a gift voucher for the total amount
